Lionel Messi will go down as one the greatest players of all time - arguably the best- and he has created many unbelievable moments over the years. From consistent team glory to stunning individual achievements - Messi has done it all over a long period of time. Which of Messi's achievements have truly been the most eye-catching? There is no doubt that Lionel Messi is 'Here to Create'. Having been criticised for his national team performances at the highest level, Messi responded by setting the 2014 Brazil World Cup alight. ] He scored four goals - all in the group stage - as Argentina bulldozed their way through to the final against Germany. Unfortunately Argentina were beaten in extra time by a Mario Gotze goal, but Messi received the highest individual accolade - being named the tournament's best player.
